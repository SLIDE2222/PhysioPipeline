PhysioPipeline mobile Google login fix

Replace:
- api.js
- google-auth.js
- login.html
- cadastro.html
- script.js

What changed:
1. login.html and cadastro.html now show the real Google-rendered button instead of a custom fake button + hidden Google render div.
2. google-auth.js no longer skips google-hidden-render containers and explicitly saves the returned token to localStorage and sessionStorage.
3. api.js exposes auth helpers and saves auth to both storages.
4. script.js reads profile.name as well as profile.nome.

Why:
On mobile, Google Identity Services prompt() can be skipped or blocked when triggered through a custom button. The official rendered button is much more reliable.
